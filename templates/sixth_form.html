6th Grade iPad User Agreement

PLEASE NOTE:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Willmar Middle School 6th grade students will be assigned an iPad at the beginning of the school year. The iPads of 6th grade students will be checked in to the student's 5th hour class.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Please read the following information pertaining to the care and use of the iPad that is assigned to your child

*The iPad is the property of Willmar Public Schools and as a result MAY be seized and contents inspected at any time. The student should have NO expectation of privacy of materials found on an iPad.

*The App Store will not be avilable to students at the middle school. Students will only be able to download apps from the Self Service app.

*Parents and students must sign this agreement before the iPad can be issued

*Never leave an iPad unattended. When not in your personal possession, the iPad should be in a secure, locked environment. Unattended iPads will be collected and stored in the main office.

*If the iPad is lost, it must be reported to the main office as soon as possible. It may be the responsibility of the student and family to recover or replace the lost iPad. If necessary, the district and the police will assist in the recovery.

*REPLACEMENT COSTS FOR DAMAGES AND REPARIS:
-Damaged or broken screen may cost up to $50.00 depending on what layer of the screen is broken.
-Replacement cost for the provided iPad case is $30.00
-Replacement cost for the entire iPad is $400.00

*Do not attempt to gain access to the internal electronics or repair your iPad. If your iPad fails to work or is damaged, report the problem to the school main office as soon as possible.

*Students may not remove the school-issued case. Students should not decorate the iPad case.

*All electronic recordings created with the device must comply with district policies and state and federal laws. District policy prohibits the use of electronic recording devices in a manner that compromises the privacy interests of other individuals. District policy also prohibits harassment and bullying. Users of the iPad are required to use the device in a manner that complies with these and other district policies. Use of the iPad in a manner that vilates district policy may result in revocation of the device and may result in further disciplinary consequeses.

*Secret or surrepitituou recording taken without the knowledge and consent of the person or persons being recorded is not allowed. Violations of state and federal reording laws may be reported to the proper authorities and may result in criminal prosecution.

*You are NOT ALLOWED to lend your iPad to another person. Each iPad is assigned to an individual and the responsibility for the care of the iPad soley rests with that individual.

*Each student will be REQUIRED to use the first four digits of their lunch number as a passcode lock to the iPad. Students are required to use their given passcode.

*Access to technology in the Willmar Middle School has been established for educational purposes. The use of the Willmar Middle School's electronic technologies is a valued resource in our community. All electronic technologies MUST be used in support of the educational program of the district. This access MAY be revoked at any time for abusive or inappropriate conduct related to the use of electronic technologies. Failure to comply with the district's discipline policy, Internet Acceptable Use Policy, or the guildlines stated in this document for the case and use of the iPad MAY result in the loss of privilge to use the iPad at school.

These Actions are STRICTLY PROHIBITED:

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  1. Any action that violates existing board policy or public law

  2. Sending, accessing, uploading, downloading, or distributing offensive, explicit, profane, threatening, pornographic, obscene, or sexually-explicit materials

  3. Pictures, video, and audio recordings of any staff member WITHOUT written consent

  4. Pictures, video, and audio recordings of any student WITHOUT consent

  5. Pictures, video, and audio recordings of any student or staff member AT ANY TIME in locker rooms or restrooms

  6. Use of chatrooms, sites selling term papers, book reports, and other forms of student work

  7. Vandalism (any malicious attempt to harm or destory hardware, software or data, including, but not limited to, the uploading or creation of computer viruses or computer programs that can infilitrate computer systems and or damage software components) of school equipment will not be allowed

  8. Transmitting or accessing materials that are obscene, offensive, threatening, or otherwise intended to harass or demean recipients

  9. Bypassing the Willmar Public Schools web filter through a web proxy or VPN

  10. Messaging services or non-educational social metworks at school -- includes, but not limited to: Twitter, Pinterest, Facebook, Snapchat, Instagram, etc.

  11. Spamming -- sending mass or inappropriate emails

  12. Gaining access to other student's accounts, files, and or data

  13. Use of the school's internet e-mail accounts for financial or commercial gain or for any illegal activity

  14. Giving out personal information, for any reason, over the internet. This includes, but is not limited to, setting up internet accounts including those necessary for chat rooms, Ebay, email, etc.

  15. Participation in credit card fraud, electronic forgery or other forms of illegal behavior
